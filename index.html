Nick Boyce

I have been designing websites and moving images since 1997, and have been specialising on technology for the art world since 2005.

I've developed Print on Demand programs for institutions such as Tate, MoMA, The Natural History Museum, Smithsonian, the Art Institute of Chicago, as well as brands such as Disney, Next, B&Q, Selfridges and Habitat.

I'm currently Head of Web Technology at Easyart.

I'm passionate about technology and art, and have exhibited in London, Adelaide, Melbourne and Amsterdam, and was a contributor to Web Graphics for Non-Designers for Glasshaus Publishing.
